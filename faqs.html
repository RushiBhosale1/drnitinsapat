General Information

We offer pediatric dentistry, preventive dental checkups, oral and maxillofacial treatments, sedation dentistry, and treatment for special health care needs.

We recommend regular dental checkups every 6 months for children and adults. For children with special needs, we may recommend more frequent visits based on individual requirements.

Please contact us to discuss insurance options and payment plans. We strive to make dental care accessible for all families.

During your first visit, we'll conduct a comprehensive examination, discuss your child's dental history, and create a personalized treatment plan. We take time to make children comfortable and explain procedures in a child-friendly manner.

Pediatric Care

Pediatric dental care focuses on the oral health of children from infancy through adolescence. We provide gentle, child-friendly dental care with specialized attention to children's unique dental needs.

Children should visit the dentist every 6 months for regular checkups. For children with special needs or higher risk of cavities, we may recommend more frequent visits.

Yes, we offer conscious and deep sedation options for children who may be anxious or need extensive treatment. Our sedation options help ensure a comfortable dental experience.

We provide specialized care for children with special health care needs. Our team is trained to handle various conditions with patience, understanding, and appropriate treatment modifications.

Our clinic features a gentle, child-friendly environment with experienced staff who know how to make children comfortable. We explain procedures in simple terms and use techniques to reduce anxiety.

Sedation Dentistry

Sedation dentistry uses medication to help patients relax during dental procedures. We offer local anesthesia, conscious sedation, and general anesthesia options for patient comfort.

We offer local anesthesia for numbing, conscious sedation for relaxation, and general anesthesia for complex procedures. The type depends on the procedure and patient needs.

Yes, sedation is safe when administered by experienced professionals. We carefully evaluate each child and use age-appropriate sedation techniques with proper monitoring.

Sedation is recommended for anxious patients, children with special needs, complex procedures, or patients with strong gag reflexes. We assess each case individually.

After sedation, patients may feel drowsy and need rest. We provide detailed post-sedation care instructions and ensure you have a responsible adult to accompany you home.

Oral & Maxillofacial

Oral and maxillofacial treatment addresses complex dental and facial conditions. We provide comprehensive care for jaw problems, facial pain, and complex dental procedures.

We treat jaw disorders, facial pain, complex extractions, dental implants, and reconstructive procedures. Our expertise covers both routine and complex oral surgery cases.

Yes, we offer dental implants and implant-supported dentures. Our team has extensive experience in implant placement and restoration for optimal results.

Recovery varies by procedure. We provide detailed post-operative instructions and follow-up care. Most patients return to normal activities within a few days to weeks.

Yes, we specialize in treating children with special health care needs. Our team is trained to provide gentle, patient care with appropriate modifications for various conditions.
